🎮 How to Play

Your Mission

Navigate through ancient temples, find keys, unlock doors, and collect artifacts!

Game Controls

Arrow Keys
Move your character
I key
Interact with objects
R Key
Restart level
H Key
Show hint

Game Elements

🧱 Walls - Cannot pass through
⚙️ Levers - Activate to open paths
🔑 Keys - Unlock doors
🚪 Doors - Require keys to open
💎 Artifacts - Collect to complete levels
🪨 Stones - Can be pushed

How to Win

  1. Explore each room thoroughly
  2. Find keys and artifacts
  3. Activate levers to open paths
  4. Unlock doors with keys
  5. Collect all artifacts
  6. Find the golden exit
